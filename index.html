Property Overview

Scale, Privacy, and South Side Convenience.

Hidden within the lush hillsides of Lawai Valley, this nearly 20-acre property offers a rare opportunity to own a substantial stretch of Kauai’s South Side landscape.

Located between Poipu, Koloa, and Kalaheo, the property blends rolling pastureland, mature tropical greenery, mountain backdrops, and distant ocean views. Established access paths wind through open meadow clearings and gently sloping terrain, revealing multiple potential building sites surrounded by privacy and natural beauty.
